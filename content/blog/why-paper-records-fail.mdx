It's hard to believe that in 2026, many clinics still rely on physical paper files. While paper might seem reliable, the hidden costs are staggering.

### The Hidden Costs

1. **Physical Space:** A clinic with 5,000 active patients needs hundreds of square feet just for filing cabinets.
2. **Access Bottlenecks:** Only one person can view a paper file at a time.
3. **Loss and Damage:** Fire, flood, or simple misplacement can permanently destroy a patient's medical history.

<Callout type="success">
  Digitizing records immediately reclaims physical space and allows concurrent access by the entire care team.
</Callout>

### The Compliance Nightmare

In a paper world, auditing who looked at a patient's file is impossible. With OMRS, every view, edit, and export is cryptographically logged.

It's time to leave the filing cabinets behind.
